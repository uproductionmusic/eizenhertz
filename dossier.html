FILE: EIZENHERTZ_01
STATUS: ACTIVE / UNSTABLE
CATEGORY: SONIC PROJECT

Eizenhertz is not a fixed identity, but a working system.

It exists as a space for fragmented perception — where sound, memory, and meaning do not resolve into clarity, but remain in motion.

The project emerged from an interest in states that are difficult to articulate directly: tension without resolution, connection without certainty, and repetition that does not lead to familiarity.

Rather than telling a linear story, Eizenhertz collects traces. Small structures of thought, emotional residue, and sonic patterns that repeat without settling into a single interpretation.

There is no stable “about” behind this work. Only shifting positions — sometimes distant, sometimes immediate — held together by continuity rather than explanation.

What appears here is not a self-portrait, but a dossier: an incomplete record of something still forming.
